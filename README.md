## ✨ What Makes Sensai Special?

Sensai isn't just a quiz app—it's a personalized learning experience driven by an intelligent ecosystem.

* 🗣️ **Interactive AI Avatar:** Learn alongside your personal AI Sensei! The avatar guides you through your journey, provides real-time feedback, and brings your quizzes to life.
* 🧠 **Smart Recommendations:** Sensai analyzes your performance and tailors the next topics directly to your skill level.
* 🎧 **Immersive TTS (Text-to-Speech):** Get questions, answers, and avatar feedback read aloud for a fully accessible and engaging experience.
* 📊 **In-Depth Quiz History:** Track your progress over time and see exactly where you need to improve.
* 🎨 **Stunning UI:** Crafted beautifully with Jetpack Compose for fluid animations and a sleek, modern design.
